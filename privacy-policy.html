Privacy Policy – NSE Hunter

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NSE Hunter ("the App") handles user information.

1. No Data Collection

NSE Hunter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The app does not require you to create an account, and no information about you or your device is sent to any server.

2. Purchase Information

One-time purchase status is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to or accessible by the developer.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

This app contains no analytics tools, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

4. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
